Have you ever wanted to share a link and see how many times it have been clicked? Then you have come to the right place! Clickcount offers and easy solution to this matter.

Clickcount is a public web service which allows anyone to wrap an arbitrary URL around one generated from our servers.
The generated URL will immediately forward the request to the source URL while updating a the click counter.

Clickcount can also minify URLs, making them more appealing to look at.
